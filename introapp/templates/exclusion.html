Exclusion

Exclusion is the deliberate act of leaving you out, and can happen in many ways, some of which are:

1. You might be excluded from parties or activities.

2. Your friends are having online conversations and tagging other friends but not you.

3. You are not using social networking sites or do not have a smartphone and are deliberately excluded from conversations by others because of this.
