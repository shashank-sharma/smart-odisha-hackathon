I am being Cyberbullied - How can I stop it?


Know what you are looking for

Always be on the lookout for fraping, cyberstalking, harrassment and impersonation. Cyberbullies do not attack with a notice. An person can be victimised anywhere and anytime. It does not even matter if the victim and the bully are acquainted or not.
It is therefore important to know and understand the different types of cyberbullying.

To know more about the different types of cyberbulling, visit: Types of Cyberbullying

Take immediate action

Attempt to identify the cause, stop responding to the bully's message. Save evidence in the form of screenshots or recordings before blocking the bully. After blocking the bully, do not forget to change your account settings.
We will provide you with brief counseling and you can also submit your complaint.

To know more, visit: Report Complaint

Get outside help

Always remeber: Do not hesitate and wait too long to ask for help. This can lead to disastrous situations. If you are a child or a teenager, ask an adult for help. Often, talking it out with parents and teachers is the best solution. You can also report the bully to your service providers if he is taking the telecommunication way to bully you. If the problem is too large to be handled, then it is best to get the law enforcement involved. This is where this portal comes into play.
In case you have any doubt regarding the web portal, visit: FAQ Pages
